# Flux Advanced Guide

## Introduction

Flux can generate highly detailed and realistic images when you write structured prompts and use consistent styles. This guide covers advanced techniques for achieving professional-quality results.

---

# Advanced Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Lighting → Camera Angle → Style → Mood → Quality

### Example

A futuristic female pilot standing beside a sleek spaceship on a rainy neon-lit runway, cinematic lighting, low-angle shot, cyberpunk style, ultra realistic, highly detailed, 8K.

---

# Character Consistency

- Keep the same character description.
- Use the same clothing and hairstyle.
- Reuse colors and accessories.
- Save prompts that produce good results.

---

# Composition

- Use foreground, middle ground, and background.
- Keep the main subject in focus.
- Avoid cluttered scenes.
- Balance the visual elements.

---

# Lighting Techniques

- Golden Hour
- Blue Hour
- Studio Lighting
- Cinematic Lighting
- Volumetric Lighting
- Neon Lighting

---

# Camera Angles

- Close-up
- Medium Shot
- Wide Shot
- Low Angle
- High Angle
- Bird's Eye View
- Over-the-Shoulder

---

# Quality Tips

- Write detailed prompts.
- Keep styles consistent.
- Generate multiple variations.
- Refine prompts step by step.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Mixing too many art styles.
- Missing lighting details.
- Weak subject descriptions.
- Overly complicated prompts.

---

# Pro Tips

- Build your own prompt library.
- Study photography for better composition.
- Save successful prompts for future projects.
- Practice with different styles and lighting.
