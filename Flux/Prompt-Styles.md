# Flux Prompt Styles

## Introduction

Choosing the right art style helps Flux generate images that match your creative vision. Below are some of the most popular styles.

---

# Realistic

Perfect for lifelike portraits, landscapes, and product photography.

**Example:**

A realistic portrait of a young woman with natural lighting, highly detailed skin, DSLR photography.

---

# Cinematic

Inspired by movie scenes with dramatic lighting and composition.

**Example:**

A lone warrior standing on a mountain at sunset, cinematic lighting, epic atmosphere.

---

# Anime

Bright colors, expressive characters, and Japanese animation aesthetics.

**Example:**

A cheerful anime girl under cherry blossom trees, vibrant colors, detailed anime style.

---

# Fantasy

Magic, mythical creatures, enchanted forests, and legendary worlds.

**Example:**

A dragon flying above a magical castle, glowing sky, fantasy art.

---

# Cyberpunk

Neon lights, futuristic cities, advanced technology, rainy streets.

**Example:**

A cyberpunk hacker walking through a neon-lit city at night, cinematic composition.

---

# Pixar Style

Colorful 3D animated characters with expressive faces.

**Example:**

A smiling cartoon fox wearing a backpack, Pixar-style, vibrant lighting.

---

# Digital Art

Modern illustrations with clean details and vibrant colors.

---

# Concept Art

Professional artwork used for films, games, and creative design.

---

# Best Practices

- Choose one primary style.
- Match the style with the subject.
- Keep the lighting consistent.
- Avoid mixing unrelated styles.
