# Flux Beginner Guide

## Introduction

Flux is a powerful AI image generation model known for producing highly realistic, detailed, and creative images from text prompts. It is ideal for concept art, portraits, product photography, landscapes, and cinematic scenes.

---

# Getting Started

To generate better images:

- Describe the main subject clearly.
- Add the environment.
- Specify the lighting.
- Mention the camera angle.
- Choose an art style.
- Include quality keywords.

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

Subject + Action + Environment + Lighting + Camera Angle + Style + Quality

---

# Example Prompt

A futuristic soldier standing on a rooftop overlooking a neon city, cinematic lighting, low-angle shot, ultra realistic, highly detailed, 8K.

---

# Popular Styles

- Realistic
- Cinematic
- Anime
- Fantasy
- Cyberpunk
- Pixar
- Digital Art
- Concept Art

---

# Tips

- Keep prompts clear and specific.
- Use realistic lighting descriptions.
- Focus on one main subject.
- Experiment with different styles.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Conflicting styles.
- Missing lighting details.
- Overloaded descriptions.

---

# Practice Prompt

A majestic white tiger walking through a misty forest at sunrise, golden hour lighting, cinematic composition, ultra realistic, highly detailed.
