# Complete Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Camera Movement → Lighting → Style → Mood → Quality

### Example

A futuristic astronaut walking through a glowing cyberpunk city at night, slow cinematic dolly shot, volumetric lighting, dramatic atmosphere, ultra realistic, 4K.

---

# Professional Workflow

1. Plan the scene.
2. Write a detailed prompt.
3. Select the visual style.
4. Define camera movement.
5. Add realistic lighting.
6. Generate multiple versions.
7. Compare the results.
8. Refine the prompt.
9. Export the best video.

---

# Best Practices

- Use detailed prompts.
- Keep the subject consistent.
- Describe realistic camera movement.
- Match lighting with the scene.
- Generate multiple variations before choosing the final result.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Missing camera movement.
- Weak lighting descriptions.
- Mixing too many styles.
- Unrealistic expectations.

---

# Pro Tips

- Build a prompt library.
- Save successful prompts.
- Keep character appearance consistent.
- Use cinematic terminology.
- Refine prompts gradually.

# Conclusion

With strong prompts, thoughtful camera movement, and consistent practice, Higgsfield can produce professional AI videos suitable for YouTube, films, advertising, and social media content.
