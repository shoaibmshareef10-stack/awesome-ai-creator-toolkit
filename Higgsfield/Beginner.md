
# Higgsfield Beginner Guide

## What is Higgsfield?

Higgsfield is an AI-powered video generation platform that helps creators produce cinematic videos from text prompts and images with realistic motion and camera control.

---

# Key Features

- Text to Video
- Image to Video
- Cinematic Camera Motion
- AI Character Animation
- Realistic Lighting
- High-Quality Video Generation

---

# Getting Started

1. Create a Higgsfield account.
2. Select a video generation model.
3. Write a detailed prompt.
4. Generate your video.
5. Refine and export the best result.

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

Subject + Action + Environment + Camera + Lighting + Style

### Example

A young explorer walking through an ancient jungle temple, cinematic lighting, slow dolly shot, ultra realistic, 4K.

---

# Tips

- Write detailed prompts.
- Use cinematic camera movements.
- Describe the lighting.
- Keep the subject consistent.
- Generate multiple variations.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Short prompts.
- No camera movement.
- Missing lighting.
- Conflicting styles.
