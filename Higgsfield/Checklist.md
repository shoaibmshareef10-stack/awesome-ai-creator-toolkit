# Higgsfield Checklist

## Before Generating

- Clear subject
- Clear action
- Detailed environment
- Camera movement included
- Lighting described
- Art style mentioned
- High quality requested

---

# Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Camera → Lighting → Style → Quality

---

## During Generation

- Check character consistency
- Check camera movement
- Check lighting
- Check realism
- Generate multiple variations

---

## After Generation

- Review the video
- Improve weak prompts
- Refine important details
- Export the best version

---

# Common Mistakes

- Short prompts
- Missing lighting
- No camera movement
- Conflicting styles
- Unrealistic descriptions

---

# Pro Tips

- Keep prompts detailed.
- Use cinematic camera movements.
- Maintain character consistency.
- Save your best prompts.
- Test multiple prompt variations.
