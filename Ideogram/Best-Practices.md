# Ideogram Best Practices

## Introduction

Following best practices helps you create high-quality AI images with better composition, accurate typography, and professional results.

---

# Write Detailed Prompts

Describe the subject, style, lighting, colors, composition, and quality clearly.

---

# Keep Typography Simple

Use short, clear text for logos, posters, and social media graphics. Avoid long sentences.

---

# Choose the Right Style

Select a style that matches your project.

Examples:
- Photorealistic
- Minimalist
- 3D Render
- Digital Art
- Watercolor
- Cartoon
- Anime

---

# Use Good Lighting

Mention lighting such as:

- Soft Lighting
- Studio Lighting
- Golden Hour
- Neon Lighting
- Dramatic Lighting

---

# Select a Color Palette

Include colors in your prompt.

Examples:

- Blue and White
- Black and Gold
- Pastel Colors
- Neon Colors
- Warm Tones

---

# Generate Multiple Variations

Create several versions and compare the results before choosing the best one.

---

# Refine Your Prompts

Improve one element at a time instead of changing everything.

---

# Save Your Best Prompts

Build a personal prompt library for faster and more consistent results.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts
- Conflicting styles
- Unclear typography
- Missing lighting
- Ignoring composition

---

# Final Tip

Practice regularly and keep refining your prompts. Small improvements often lead to significantly better AI-generated images.
