# Ideogram Advanced Guide

## Introduction

Ideogram is designed for creating professional AI-generated images with exceptional text rendering, creative typography, and high-quality visuals. With advanced prompting techniques, you can produce logos, posters, advertisements, product mockups, and social media graphics.

---

# Advanced Prompt Formula

Subject → Details → Environment → Style → Lighting → Color Palette → Composition → Quality

### Example

A luxury perfume advertisement featuring an elegant glass bottle on a marble surface, soft golden lighting, premium product photography, realistic reflections, minimalist background, ultra detailed, 8K.

# Typography Tips

- Use short and clear text.
- Choose readable fonts.
- Match typography with the design style.
- Leave enough spacing around text.
- Use high contrast for better visibility.

---

# Quality Tips

- Write detailed prompts.
- Mention the desired style.
- Specify lighting and colors.
- Generate multiple variations.
- Refine prompts step by step.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Overloading prompts.
- Using conflicting styles.
- Ignoring composition.
- Writing unclear text instructions.

---

# Pro Tips

- Save your best prompts.
- Test different aspect ratios.
- Experiment with typography.
- Build a reusable prompt library.
