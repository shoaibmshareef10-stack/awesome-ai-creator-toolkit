# Ideogram Prompt Styles Guide

## Introduction

Ideogram supports a wide variety of artistic styles. Choosing the right style helps create images that match your creative vision.

---

# Photorealistic

Creates highly realistic images with natural lighting and lifelike details.

Example:
A luxury living room with modern furniture, photorealistic, natural sunlight, ultra detailed.

---

# Minimalist

Simple composition with clean shapes and limited colors.

Example:
A minimalist coffee logo with elegant typography on a white background.

---

# 3D Render

Produces realistic three-dimensional objects with depth and reflections.

Example:
A futuristic robot standing in a neon city, 3D render, cinematic lighting.

---

# Digital Art

Perfect for concept art, illustrations, and creative designs.

Example:
A magical forest filled with glowing plants, digital art, vibrant colors.

---

# Watercolor

Soft brush strokes with artistic textures.

Example:
A colorful hummingbird sitting on flowers, watercolor painting.

---

# Oil Painting

Classic painted look with rich textures.

Example:
A royal king sitting on a golden throne, oil painting style.

---

# Cartoon & Anime

Stylized illustrations with expressive characters.

Example:
A cheerful schoolgirl walking under cherry blossom trees, anime style.

---

# Typography Design

Best for logos, posters, quotes, and social media graphics with clear text rendering.

Example:
A modern motivational poster with bold typography, blue and white theme.

---

# Pro Tips

- Match the style to your project.
- Keep prompts clear and detailed.
- Mention lighting and colors.
- Generate multiple variations.
- Save your favorite prompts.
