
# Ideogram Beginner Guide

## What is Ideogram?

Ideogram is an AI image generation tool known for creating high-quality images with excellent text rendering. It is widely used for logos, posters, social media graphics, illustrations, and creative artwork.

---

# Key Features

- AI Image Generation
- Excellent Text Rendering
- Multiple Art Styles
- Logo Design
- Poster Creation
- Typography Support
- High Resolution Images

---

# Getting Started

1. Open Ideogram.
2. Sign in to your account.
3. Enter a detailed prompt.
4. Choose an aspect ratio or style.
5. Generate the image.
6. Download your favorite result.

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

Subject → Details → Style → Lighting → Quality

### Example

A modern coffee shop logo with elegant typography, minimal design, warm colors, clean background, high quality.

---

# Beginner Tips

- Write clear prompts.
- Keep prompts specific.
- Mention your preferred style.
- Use descriptive lighting.
- Generate multiple variations.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Using very short prompts.
- Giving conflicting instructions.
- Ignoring style descriptions.
- Forgetting image quality.
