# Veo Character Consistency Guide

## Introduction

Character consistency ensures that the same character looks and behaves identically across every generated scene. Consistent appearance is essential for storytelling and professional AI filmmaking.

---

# Identity

Always keep the following unchanged:

- Face shape
- Hair style
- Hair color
- Eye color
- Skin tone
- Age
- Gender
- Body type

---

# Outfit Consistency

Describe the complete outfit every time.

Include:

- Shirt
- Pants
- Shoes
- Jacket
- Hat
- Glasses
- Watch
- Jewelry
- Backpack
- Other accessories

---

# Facial Details

Always specify:

- Face shape
- Hair style
- Beard or clean shave
- Eye color
- Eyebrows
- Smile
- Facial expression

---

# Body Language

Examples:

- Confident standing posture
- Natural walking
- Relaxed shoulders
- Realistic hand gestures
- Natural eye contact
- Balanced body movement

---

# Emotional Expressions

Examples:

- Happy
- Sad
- Angry
- Fear
- Shock
- Excited
- Determined
- Calm
- Serious
- Confident

---

---

# Example

25-year-old athletic man, oval face, short black hair, brown eyes, clean shave, wearing a black hoodie, dark cargo pants, white sneakers, black smartwatch, calm confident expression, natural posture, realistic body movement.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Changing hairstyle
- Changing outfit colors
- Different facial structure
- Different body proportions
- Different accessories
- Different age

---

# Pro Tips

- Repeat the complete character description in every prompt.
- Never change the outfit unless the story requires it.
- Keep accessories identical.
- Maintain realistic facial expressions.
- Use the same body language throughout the sequence.
