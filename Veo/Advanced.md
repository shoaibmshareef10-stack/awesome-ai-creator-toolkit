# Veo Advanced Prompt Guide

## Advanced Prompt Structure

Subject + Action + Environment + Camera + Lighting + Mood + Style + Audio + Quality

### Example

Subject:
A futuristic warrior in silver armor

Action:
Runs through a burning cyberpunk city while explosions happen

Environment:
Rainy neon city at night, smoke, flying vehicles

Camera:
Low angle tracking shot, cinematic dolly in

Lighting:
Blue and orange cinematic lighting, volumetric fog

Mood:
Epic, intense, dramatic

Style:
Ultra realistic, Hollywood blockbuster

Audio:
Heavy cinematic music, thunder, explosions

Quality:
8K, HDR, photorealistic, highly detailed
