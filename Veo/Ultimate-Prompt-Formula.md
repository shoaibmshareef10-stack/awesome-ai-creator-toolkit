# Complete Prompt Template

## Subject

Who or what is the main focus?

---

## Character

Describe:

- Face
- Hair
- Eyes
- Outfit
- Accessories
- Body Type
- Expression

---

## Action

Describe exactly what the character is doing.

---

## Emotion

Examples:

- Happy
- Angry
- Determined
- Emotional
- Fearless
- Excited

---

## Environment

Include:

- Location
- Background
- Time
- Weather
- Atmosphere

---

## Camera

Include:

- Angle
- Movement
- Lens
- Speed

---

## Lighting

Describe:

- Light source
- Direction
- Shadow style
- Mood

---

## Visual Style

Examples:

- Hollywood

- Fantasy

# Common Mistakes

- Prompts are too short.
- Character details change.
- Camera movement is missing.
- Lighting is not described.
- Conflicting instructions.
- Environment lacks detail.

---

# Best Practices

- Keep prompts clear and specific.
- Reuse the same character description.
- Match lighting to the story.
- Use one main camera movement.
- Add environment details for realism.
- Save your best prompt templates.
