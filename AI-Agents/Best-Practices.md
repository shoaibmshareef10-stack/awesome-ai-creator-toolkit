# 🤖 AI Agents - Best Practices

## Define Clear Goals

Always give your AI agent a clear objective before starting.

---

## Provide Context

Include all necessary background information.

---

## Break Large Tasks

Split complex tasks into smaller steps.

---

## Use Reliable Tools

Connect only trusted APIs and external services.

---

## Verify Results

Always review AI-generated outputs for accuracy.

---

## Protect Sensitive Data

Avoid sharing passwords, API keys, or confidential information.

---

## Optimize Prompts

Use specific instructions instead of vague requests.

---

## Test Multiple Approaches

Experiment with different prompts and workflows.

---

## Monitor Performance

Track response quality, speed, and costs.

---

## Keep Improving

Continuously refine prompts and agent workflows.
