# Midjourney Best Practices

## Introduction

Following best practices helps you generate high-quality, consistent, and professional AI images.

## Best Practices

### 1. Write Clear Prompts

Describe the subject, environment, lighting, camera angle, style, and quality.

---

### 2. Use Good Lighting

Examples:
- Cinematic Lighting
- Golden Hour
- Studio Lighting
- Volumetric Lighting

---

### 3. Select the Right Camera Angle

Examples:
- Close-up
- Wide Shot
- Low Angle
- High Angle
- Eye Level

---

### 4. Use Appropriate Aspect Ratio

- 1:1 → Instagram Posts
- 9:16 → Shorts & Reels
- 16:9 → YouTube Thumbnails & Wallpapers
- 4:5 → Social Media Posts

---

### 5. Keep Prompts Organized

Recommended order:

Subject → Action → Environment → Lighting → Camera → Style → Quality → Parameters

---

### 6. Experiment Carefully

Change only one parameter at a time to understand its effect.

---

### 7. Save Your Best Prompts

Create a personal prompt library for future projects.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts
- Too many conflicting styles
- Missing lighting
- Wrong aspect ratio
- Using excessive parameters

---

## Pro Tips

- Use reference images when possible.
- Test multiple prompt versions.
- Build prompt templates.
- Review results before making changes.
