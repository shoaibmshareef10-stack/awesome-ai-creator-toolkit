# Midjourney Camera Angles

## Introduction

Camera angles dramatically change the storytelling, emotion, and visual impact of an image.

## Popular Camera Angles

### Eye Level
Natural and realistic perspective.

### Close-Up
Highlights facial expressions and small details.

### Extreme Close-Up
Focuses on a specific feature such as eyes or hands.

### Medium Shot
Shows the subject from the waist up.

### Full Body Shot
Displays the entire subject.

### Wide Shot
Captures the subject and surrounding environment.

### Low Angle
Makes the subject appear powerful and dominant.

### High Angle
Makes the subject appear smaller or more vulnerable.

### Bird's Eye View
Top-down perspective for dramatic scenes.

### Worm's Eye View
Ground-level perspective looking upward.

### Over-the-Shoulder
Common for conversations and cinematic storytelling.

### Drone View
Perfect for landscapes, cities, and large environments.

## Camera Lens Examples

- 24mm – Wide landscape
- 35mm – Street photography
- 50mm – Natural perspective
- 85mm – Portraits
- 200mm – Wildlife and sports

## Example Prompt

A futuristic warrior, low-angle shot, cinematic lighting, 85mm lens, ultra realistic, highly detailed.

## Best Practices

- Match the camera angle with your scene.
- Use portrait lenses for characters.
- Use wide lenses for environments.
- Combine camera angle with proper lighting.
