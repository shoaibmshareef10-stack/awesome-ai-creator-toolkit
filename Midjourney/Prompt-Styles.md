# Midjourney Prompt Styles

## Introduction

Prompt styles define the artistic look and feel of your generated image. Choosing the right style helps create unique, consistent, and professional results.

## Popular Styles

### Realistic

Ultra realistic, photorealistic, highly detailed, DSLR quality.

### Cinematic

Movie scene, cinematic lighting, dramatic atmosphere, anamorphic lens.

### Anime

Anime style, vibrant colors, expressive characters, Japanese animation.

### Pixar

3D animated character, Pixar style, colorful, soft lighting.

### Disney

Disney animation style, magical environment, expressive faces.

### Comic Book

Comic book illustration, bold outlines, dynamic action, vibrant colors.

### Cyberpunk

Neon lights, futuristic city, rain, glowing reflections.

### Fantasy

Magic, dragons, castles, mystical forest, epic atmosphere.

### Steampunk

Victorian technology, gears, brass machines, industrial design.

### Minimalist

Clean composition, simple colors, modern design.

## Combining Styles

You can combine multiple styles.

Example:

Realistic portrait, cinematic lighting, cyberpunk background, ultra detailed.

## Best Practices

- Choose one main style.
- Match lighting with the style.
- Keep prompts clean.
- Test different artistic combinations.

## Common Mistakes

- Mixing too many styles.
- Contradicting keywords.
- Forgetting lighting.
- Ignoring composition.
