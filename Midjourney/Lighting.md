# Midjourney Lighting Guide

## Introduction

Lighting is one of the most important elements in AI image generation. It defines the mood, realism, depth, and cinematic quality of an image.

## Popular Lighting Types

### Cinematic Lighting
Perfect for movie scenes with dramatic shadows.

### Golden Hour
Warm sunlight during sunrise or sunset.

### Blue Hour
Soft blue tones after sunset.

### Studio Lighting
Clean, balanced lighting for portraits and products.

### Soft Lighting
Smooth shadows and natural skin tones.

### Hard Lighting
Strong contrast with sharp shadows.

### Rim Lighting
Creates a glowing outline around the subject.

### Neon Lighting
Colorful futuristic lighting for cyberpunk scenes.

### Volumetric Lighting
Visible light rays through fog, smoke, or windows.

### Moonlight
Cool blue nighttime lighting.

## Best Practices

- Match lighting with the mood.
- Combine lighting with camera angles.
- Use realistic light sources.
- Avoid mixing too many lighting styles.

## Example Prompts

### Portrait

Beautiful woman, soft studio lighting, realistic skin, 85mm lens, ultra detailed.

### Fantasy

Ancient castle, volumetric lighting, magical atmosphere, cinematic composition.

### Cyberpunk

Futuristic street, neon lighting, rain, reflections, ultra realistic.

## Common Mistakes

- No lighting description.
- Contradictory lighting styles.
- Overusing dramatic effects.

## Pro Tips

- Add lighting before camera parameters.
- Use volumetric lighting for cinematic scenes.
- Combine golden hour with landscapes.
- Use studio lighting for product photography.
