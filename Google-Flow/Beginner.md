# Google Flow Beginner Prompts

Welcome to the Google Flow prompt collection.

## Prompt 1 – Cinematic Character Introduction

Create a cinematic hero introduction with realistic lighting, detailed environment, expressive facial emotions, natural body language, smooth camera movement, and blockbuster movie quality. Keep visuals ultra-realistic and highly detailed.

## Prompt 2 – Environment Prompt

Generate a highly detailed environment with realistic textures, cinematic lighting, volumetric fog, natural shadows, depth of field, and immersive atmosphere.
