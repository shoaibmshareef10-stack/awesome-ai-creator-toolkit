# Complete Prompt Template

Subject:
Who or what is the main focus?

Character:
Appearance, clothing, accessories, hairstyle, facial features.

Action:
What is happening?

Emotion:
Happy, serious, excited, dramatic, etc.

Environment:
Location, weather, atmosphere, background.

Camera:
Angle, movement, lens.

Lighting:
Natural, cinematic, volumetric, rim light, etc.

Style:
Realistic, Pixar, Anime, Cyberpunk, Documentary, Hollywood.

Effects:
Smoke, rain, sparks, particles, reflections, fog.

Quality:
Ultra realistic, HDR, 8K, highly detailed.

Output:
16:9 or 9:16, desired duration.

# Example Prompt

A confident superhero wearing a black futuristic suit stands on a skyscraper rooftop at sunset overlooking a cyberpunk city. Slow dolly-in camera with a 50mm cinematic lens. Warm golden-hour lighting with volumetric fog and realistic reflections. Determined facial expression and natural body posture. Hollywood blockbuster style, ultra realistic, HDR, 8K, highly detailed, 16:9.

# Common Mistakes

- Too little detail.
- Conflicting instructions.
- Missing camera information.
- No lighting description.
- Inconsistent character description.

---

# Best Practices

- Use clear and descriptive language.
- Keep character details consistent.
- Match lighting to the environment.
- Specify camera movement.
- Reuse successful prompt templates.
