# Lighting Guide

## Introduction

Lighting controls the mood, realism, and cinematic quality of AI-generated videos. Choosing the correct lighting style can completely transform a scene.

---

# Lighting Formula

Light Source + Direction + Intensity + Color + Shadows + Mood

---

# Natural Lighting

### Sunrise
- Soft orange light
- Calm atmosphere
- Long shadows

### Morning
- Bright natural sunlight
- Fresh look
- Soft shadows

### Noon
- Strong sunlight
- High brightness
- Sharp shadows

### Sunset
- Golden-hour lighting
- Warm colors
- Cinematic mood

### Moonlight
- Cool blue lighting
- Soft contrast
- Peaceful atmosphere
