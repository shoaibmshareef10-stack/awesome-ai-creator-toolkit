# Character Consistency Guide

## Introduction

Character consistency is one of the most important factors in AI video generation. Every scene should preserve the same identity, appearance, clothing, proportions, and personality.

---

# Identity

Always keep:

- Same face
- Same hairstyle
- Same skin tone
- Same age
- Same gender
- Same body type

---

# Clothing

Always describe:

- Shirt
- Pants
- Shoes
- Jacket
- Hat
- Glasses
- Watch
- Accessories

Example:

White oversized hoodie, black cargo pants, white sneakers, black smartwatch, silver chain.

---

# Face Description

Include:

- Face shape
- Hair style
- Hair color
- Eye color
- Beard or clean shave
- Facial expressions

---

# Body Language

Describe:

- Standing posture
- Walking style
- Hand movements
- Head movement
- Eye contact

---

# Expressions

Examples:

- Happy
- Angry
- Serious
- Confident
- Emotional
- Fear
- Shock
- Smile

---

Character:

---

# Example

Character:
25-year-old confident man, athletic build, short black hair, brown eyes, clean shave, wearing a white hoodie, black cargo pants, white sneakers, black smartwatch, confident facial expression, natural body language.

---

# Pro Tips

- Never change the outfit unless the story requires it.
- Keep the face identical across all scenes.
- Use the same hairstyle and accessories.
- Repeat the complete character description in every prompt.
- Maintain consistent emotions and proportions.
