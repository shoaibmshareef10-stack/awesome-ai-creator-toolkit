# Nano Banana Prompt Checklist

Before generating an image, check:

- Subject is clearly described
- Style is mentioned
- Lighting is included
- Camera angle is specified
- Background is defined
- Colors are mentioned
- Mood is included
- Composition is clear
- Quality keywords are added
- Prompt has no conflicting instructions

## Quick Formula

Subject + Style + Lighting + Camera + Background + Quality

## Quality Checklist

- Ultra realistic
- Highly detailed
- 8K quality
- HDR
- Sharp focus
- Cinematic

## Final Review

✔ Clear prompt

✔ Proper details

✔ No unnecessary words

✔ Easy to understand

✔ Ready to generate
