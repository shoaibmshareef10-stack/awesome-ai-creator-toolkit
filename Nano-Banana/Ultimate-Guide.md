# Nano Banana Ultimate Guide

## Introduction

Nano Banana is an AI image generation tool capable of creating high-quality visuals from text prompts.

---

## Workflow

1. Write a clear prompt.
2. Choose an art style.
3. Add lighting details.
4. Specify camera angle.
5. Describe the background.
6. Include quality keywords.
7. Generate multiple versions.
8. Refine the best result.

---

## Prompt Formula

Subject + Style + Lighting + Camera + Background + Quality

---

## Best Quality Keywords

- Ultra realistic
- Highly detailed
- 8K
- HDR
- Sharp focus
- Cinematic
- Professional
- Masterpiece

---

## Tips

- Keep prompts specific.
- Use descriptive words.
- Avoid conflicting instructions.
- Experiment with different styles.
- Save successful prompts.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts
- Missing lighting
- Missing background
- Too many unrelated keywords
- Unrealistic expectations

---

## Final Thoughts

The best results come from practice, experimentation, and continuously improving your prompts.
