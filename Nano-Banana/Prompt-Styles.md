# Nano Banana Prompt Styles

## Introduction

Choosing the right art style helps Nano Banana generate images that match your creative vision. Different styles create different moods, details, and artistic effects.

---

# Realistic

Creates lifelike images with natural lighting, textures, and accurate details.

**Example:**

A realistic portrait of a young woman, natural lighting, DSLR photography, ultra detailed.

---

# Cinematic

Inspired by movie scenes with dramatic lighting and composition.

**Example:**

A lone warrior standing on a mountain during sunset, cinematic lighting, epic atmosphere.

---

# Anime

Bright colors, expressive characters, and Japanese animation aesthetics.

**Example:**

A cheerful anime girl under cherry blossom trees, vibrant colors, highly detailed anime style.

---

# Fantasy

Perfect for magical worlds, mythical creatures, and enchanted landscapes.

**Example:**

A dragon flying above an ancient castle, glowing sky, fantasy art, ultra detailed.

---

# Cyberpunk

Neon lights, futuristic technology, rainy streets, and glowing cities.

**Example:**

A cyberpunk hacker walking through a neon-lit city, cinematic lighting, futuristic atmosphere.

---

# Pixar Style

Colorful 3D animated characters with expressive faces.

**Example:**

A smiling cartoon panda wearing a backpack, Pixar-style, vibrant lighting.

---

# Digital Art

Modern illustrations with vibrant colors and clean details.

---

# Watercolor

Soft artistic paintings with blended colors and natural brush strokes.

---

# Best Practices

- Choose one primary style.
- Match the style to the subject.
- Keep lighting consistent.
- Avoid mixing unrelated styles.
