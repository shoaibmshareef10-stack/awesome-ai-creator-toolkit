# Nano Banana Best Practices

## 1. Write Clear Prompts
Describe the subject clearly and avoid vague instructions.

## 2. Add Important Details
Mention style, lighting, camera angle, colors, and background.

## 3. Use Quality Keywords
Examples:
- Ultra realistic
- 8K
- Cinematic
- HDR
- Highly detailed

## 4. Keep Prompts Organized
Subject → Style → Lighting → Camera → Background → Quality

## 5. Experiment
Generate multiple versions and refine the best result.

## 6. Avoid Conflicting Instructions
Don't mix opposite styles in one prompt.

## 7. Maintain Consistency
Reuse the same character descriptions for similar outputs.

## Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts
- Missing lighting
- Missing style
- Too many unrelated keywords
- Unrealistic expectations

## Final Tip

Practice regularly and improve prompts step by step for the best results.
