# Nano Banana Advanced Guide

## Introduction

Nano Banana offers advanced tools for creating highly detailed, consistent, and professional AI-generated images. By using structured prompts and refined techniques, you can achieve outstanding results.

---

# Advanced Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Lighting → Camera Angle → Style → Mood → Quality

### Example

A futuristic female explorer standing on a glowing bridge in a cyberpunk city, cinematic lighting, volumetric light rays, low-angle shot, ultra realistic, highly detailed, 8K.

---

# Character Consistency

- Keep the same facial features.
- Use the same clothing and accessories.
- Maintain hairstyle and colors.
- Reuse successful prompts.

---

# Composition

- Create foreground, middle ground, and background.
- Keep the main subject in focus.
- Avoid cluttered scenes.
- Balance the frame naturally.

---

# Lighting Techniques

- Golden Hour
- Blue Hour
- Studio Lighting
- Cinematic Lighting
- Volumetric Lighting
- Neon Lighting

---

# Camera Angles

- Close-up
- Medium Shot
- Wide Shot
- Low Angle
- High Angle
- Bird's Eye View
- Over-the-Shoulder

---

# Quality Tips

- Use detailed prompts.
- Keep styles consistent.
- Generate multiple variations.
- Refine prompts based on results.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Mixing different art styles.
- Missing lighting descriptions.
- Weak subject details.
- Overly complicated prompts.

---

# Pro Tips

- Build a prompt library.
- Save successful prompts.
- Study photography for better composition.
- Practice with different styles.
