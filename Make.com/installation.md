# 💻 Installation

## Overview

Before creating automations, you need to create a Make.com account, configure your workspace, and connect the applications you want to automate.

---

## Requirements

You'll need:

- A web browser
- A valid email address
- Internet connection
- Accounts for the apps you want to connect

---

## Account Setup

To get started:

1. Sign up for a Make.com account.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Log in to your dashboard.
4. Create or join an organization.
5. Set up your workspace.

---

## Initial Configuration

After signing in:

- Complete your profile.
- Configure your workspace.
- Explore available templates.
- Connect your first application.
- Review account settings.

---

## Connecting Applications

Before building workflows:

- Authorize application access.
- Grant required permissions.
- Test the connection.
- Save your configuration.

---

## Verify Installation

Ensure that:

- Your account is active.
- Your workspace is accessible.
- Applications are connected.
- Templates are available.
- You can create a new scenario.

---

## Best Practices

- Use a secure password.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Organize applications by project.
- Remove unused connections.
- Review permissions regularly.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Skipping email verification.
- Using incorrect account permissions.
- Connecting the wrong application account.
- Ignoring security settings.
- Creating duplicate connections.

---

## Summary

A properly configured Make.com account provides a solid foundation for building secure, reliable, and scalable automation workflows.
