# Kling Ultimate Guide

## Welcome

This guide combines everything you need to create professional AI videos with Kling.

---

# Step 1: Learn the Basics

- Understand the interface.
- Learn prompt writing.
- Practice simple video prompts.

---

# Step 2: Build Better Prompts

Use this formula:

Subject → Action → Environment → Lighting → Camera → Motion → Style → Quality

---

# Step 3: Character Consistency

Always keep the following consistent:

- Face
- Hairstyle
- Clothing
- Accessories
- Body proportions
- Expressions

---

# Step 4: Environment Design

Choose environments that match your story.

Examples:

- City
- Forest
- Beach
- Space
- Cyberpunk
- Fantasy
- Mountains
- Desert

---

# Step 5: Camera Movements

Master these movements:

- Static Shot
- Pan
- Tilt
- Dolly In
- Dolly Out
- Tracking Shot
- Orbit Shot
- Crane Shot

---

# Step 6: Lighting

Recommended lighting styles:

- Cinematic
- Golden Hour
- Blue Hour
- Studio Lighting
- Volumetric Lighting
- Neon Lighting
- Rim Lighting

---

# Step 7: Professional Workflow

1. Plan the scene.
2. Write a detailed prompt.
3. Keep character consistency.
4. Select lighting.
5. Choose camera movement.
6. Generate multiple versions.
7. Compare outputs.
8. Refine the prompt.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Too many actions.
- Inconsistent character appearance.
- Poor lighting descriptions.
- Unrealistic environments.

---

# Pro Tips

- Save your best prompts.
- Build a personal prompt library.
- Practice regularly.
- Study cinematic videos.
- Improve prompts step by step.
