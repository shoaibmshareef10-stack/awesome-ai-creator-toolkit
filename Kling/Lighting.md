# Kling Lighting Guide

## Introduction

Lighting is one of the most important elements in AI video generation. It controls mood, realism, depth, and cinematic quality.

---

# Popular Lighting Styles

## Cinematic Lighting

Perfect for films and dramatic storytelling.

## Golden Hour

Warm sunlight during sunrise or sunset.

## Blue Hour

Soft blue lighting after sunset.

## Studio Lighting

Professional lighting for portraits and product videos.

## Soft Lighting

Natural light with smooth shadows.

## Hard Lighting

Strong contrast with sharp shadows.

## Volumetric Lighting

Visible light rays through fog, smoke, or windows.

## Rim Lighting

Creates a glowing outline around the subject.

## Neon Lighting

Perfect for cyberpunk and futuristic scenes.

## Moonlight

Cool blue lighting for night scenes.

---

# Matching Lighting with Scenes

| Scene | Recommended Lighting |
|--------|----------------------|
| Portrait | Soft Lighting |
| Action | Cinematic Lighting |
| Product | Studio Lighting |
| Fantasy | Volumetric Lighting |
| Cyberpunk | Neon Lighting |
| Nature | Golden Hour |

---

# Example Prompt

A futuristic warrior walking through a neon city, cinematic lighting, volumetric light rays, ultra realistic, smooth camera movement.

---

# Best Practices

- Match lighting with the mood.
- Keep lighting direction consistent.
- Use natural light sources.
- Combine lighting with camera movement.

---

# Common Mistakes

- No lighting description.
- Mixing multiple lighting styles unnecessarily.
- Flat lighting with no shadows.
- Unrealistic lighting direction.

---

# Pro Tips

- Use Golden Hour for emotional scenes.
- Use Volumetric Lighting for cinematic shots.
- Use Studio Lighting for products and portraits.
- Use Neon Lighting for futuristic environments.
