# Kling Environment Design

## Introduction

A well-designed environment makes AI videos more immersive, realistic, and cinematic. The environment should support the story and match the character's actions.

---

# Types of Environments

## Urban City

Modern buildings, busy streets, traffic, neon signs, realistic atmosphere.

## Nature

Forests, mountains, rivers, waterfalls, flowers, wildlife.

## Sci-Fi

Futuristic cities, flying cars, holograms, neon lights, advanced technology.

## Fantasy

Ancient castles, magical forests, dragons, floating islands, glowing crystals.

## Historical

Ancient temples, medieval villages, royal palaces, traditional architecture.

## Space

Planets, galaxies, space stations, astronauts, colorful nebulae.

# Example Prompt

A futuristic city with glowing skyscrapers, flying cars, light rain, neon reflections, cinematic lighting, nighttime, ultra realistic.

---

# Best Practices

- Match the environment to the story.
- Add weather naturally.
- Include time of day.
- Use cinematic lighting.
- Add depth with foreground and background details.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Empty backgrounds.
- Contradictory weather.
- Poor lighting.
- Too many unrelated objects.
- No atmosphere.
