# Runway Advanced Guide

## Introduction

Runway is a professional AI video generation and editing platform. It enables creators to produce cinematic videos using text prompts, images, and advanced editing tools.

---

# Advanced Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Camera Movement → Lighting → Style → Mood → Quality

### Example

A futuristic astronaut walking through a neon-lit city at night, slow cinematic dolly shot, volumetric lighting, dramatic atmosphere, ultra realistic, 4K.

# Quality Tips

- Write detailed prompts.
- Describe camera movement.
- Keep the subject consistent.
- Use realistic lighting.
- Generate multiple variations.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Missing camera movement.
- Conflicting styles.
- Weak subject descriptions.
- No lighting details.

---

# Pro Tips

- Plan each scene before generating.
- Maintain character consistency.
- Use cinematic camera movements.
- Refine prompts step by step.
