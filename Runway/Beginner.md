# Runway Beginner Guide

## What is Runway?

Runway is an AI-powered creative platform for generating and editing videos, images, and visual effects. It helps creators produce cinematic content using simple text prompts.

---

# Key Features

- Text to Video
- Image to Video
- Video to Video
- Background Removal
- Motion Brush
- Camera Controls
- AI Video Editing
- Frame Extension

---

# Getting Started

1. Create a Runway account.
2. Choose an AI model.
3. Write a detailed prompt.
4. Generate your video.
5. Download or edit the result.

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

Subject + Action + Environment + Style + Lighting + Camera

### Example

A futuristic robot walking through a neon city at night, cinematic style, soft volumetric lighting, slow dolly shot, ultra realistic.

---

# Tips

- Use clear prompts.
- Describe the environment.
- Mention lighting.
- Add camera movement.
- Generate multiple versions.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- No lighting details.
- Missing camera movement.
- Too many conflicting styles.
