# Runway Camera Movements Guide

## Why Camera Movement Matters

Camera movement makes AI videos feel cinematic, realistic, and engaging. A good movement adds depth and emotion to every scene.

---

# Basic Camera Movements

## Static Shot
The camera stays completely still.

## Dolly In
The camera slowly moves toward the subject.

## Dolly Out
The camera slowly moves away from the subject.

## Pan Left
The camera rotates to the left.

## Pan Right
The camera rotates to the right.

## Tilt Up
The camera points upward.

## Tilt Down
The camera points downward.

## Orbit
The camera circles around the subject.

## Crane Shot
The camera moves vertically.

## Handheld
Natural camera shake for realism.

## Tracking Shot
The camera follows the moving subject.

---

# Example Prompt

A young explorer walking through an ancient jungle temple, slow dolly in, cinematic lighting, ultra realistic, smooth camera motion.

---

# Best Practices

- Use only one or two camera movements.
- Keep movements smooth.
- Match movement with the scene.
- Avoid excessive motion.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Too many camera movements.
- Fast and unrealistic motion.
- Conflicting directions.
- Ignoring subject focus.
