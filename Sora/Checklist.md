# Sora Checklist

## Before Generating

- Clear subject
- Clear action
- Detailed environment
- Camera movement included
- Lighting described
- Style selected
- High quality requested

---

# Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Camera → Lighting → Style → Mood → Quality

---

## During Generation

- Check character consistency
- Verify camera movement
- Review lighting and shadows
- Ensure realistic motion
- Generate multiple variations

---

## After Generation

- Review the final video
- Refine weak prompts
- Improve scene details
- Export the best version

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts
- Missing camera movement
- Weak lighting descriptions
- Conflicting styles
- Unrealistic expectations

---

# Pro Tips

- Use detailed prompts.
- Keep character appearance consistent.
- Match camera movement to the scene.
- Save your best prompts.
- Test multiple prompt variations before finalizing.
