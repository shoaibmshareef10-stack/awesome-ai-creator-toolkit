# Sora Beginner Guide

## What is Sora?

Sora is OpenAI's AI video generation model that creates realistic and imaginative videos from text prompts. It can generate cinematic scenes, animations, commercials, and creative storytelling with impressive visual quality.

---

# Key Features

- Text-to-Video Generation
- Image-to-Video Support
- Cinematic Camera Motion
- Realistic Physics
- High-Quality Visuals
- Creative Storytelling
- Multiple Video Styles

---

# Getting Started

1. Open Sora.
2. Sign in to your account.
3. Enter a detailed prompt.
4. Choose your preferred settings.
5. Generate the video.
6. Review and download the best result.

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Camera → Lighting → Style → Quality

### Example

A golden retriever happily running across a sunny beach, cinematic tracking shot, warm golden-hour lighting, realistic, 4K.

---

# Beginner Tips

- Write clear and detailed prompts.
- Describe the action naturally.
- Mention the environment.
- Include camera movement.
- Specify lighting and style.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Missing camera movement.
- Conflicting instructions.
- Unrealistic expectations.
