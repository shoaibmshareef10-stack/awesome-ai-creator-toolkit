# Sora Ultimate Guide

## Introduction

Sora is OpenAI's advanced AI video generation model that transforms text prompts into realistic, cinematic, and imaginative videos. It is ideal for storytelling, advertisements, short films, social media content, product showcases, and creative visual experiences.

# Professional Workflow

1. Define your video concept.
2. Write a detailed prompt.
3. Describe the environment.
4. Choose the visual style.
5. Specify camera movement.
6. Add realistic lighting.
7. Generate multiple versions.
8. Compare the results.
9. Refine the prompt.
10. Export the best video.

---

# Best Practices

- Write clear and detailed prompts.
- Keep characters and objects consistent.
- Use cinematic camera movements.
- Describe realistic lighting.
- Test multiple prompt variations.
- Save successful prompts for future projects.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Conflicting instructions.
- Missing camera movement.
- Weak lighting descriptions.
- Unrealistic scene expectations.

---

# Pro Tips

- Build your own prompt library.
- Break complex stories into multiple scenes.
- Maintain visual consistency across scenes.
- Experiment with different camera angles.
- Refine prompts gradually for better results.

---
