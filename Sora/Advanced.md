# Sora Advanced Guide

## Introduction

Sora is an advanced AI video generation model capable of producing cinematic, realistic, and highly creative videos from detailed text prompts. Better prompts result in higher-quality videos with improved storytelling and consistency.

---

# Advanced Prompt Formula

Subject → Action → Environment → Camera → Lighting → Style → Mood → Quality

### Example

A futuristic astronaut walking through a neon-lit cyberpunk city at night, slow cinematic dolly shot, volumetric lighting, dramatic atmosphere, ultra realistic, 4K.

# Quality Tips

- Write detailed prompts.
- Keep characters consistent.
- Describe realistic environments.
- Mention camera movement.
- Generate multiple variations.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Conflicting instructions.
- Missing lighting details.
- Unrealistic camera movements.

---

# Pro Tips

- Plan each scene before generating.
- Build a reusable prompt library.
- Test different camera angles.
- Refine prompts step by step.
