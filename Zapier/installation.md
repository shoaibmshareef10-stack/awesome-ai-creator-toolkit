# 💻 Installation

## Overview

Before building automations in Zapier, you need to create an account, configure your workspace, and connect the applications you want to automate.

---

## Requirements

You'll need:

- A web browser
- A valid email address
- Internet connection
- Accounts for the apps you want to connect

---

## Account Setup

To get started:

1. Sign up for a Zapier account.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Log in to your dashboard.
4. Complete your profile.
5. Configure your workspace.

---

## Initial Configuration

After signing in:

- Explore the dashboard.
- Review available app integrations.
- Create your first Zap.
- Connect your first application.
- Check account settings.

---

## Connecting Applications

Before creating workflows:

- Authorize application access.
- Grant required permissions.
- Test each connection.
- Save your configuration.

---

## Verify Installation

Ensure that:

- Your account is active.
- Your dashboard is accessible.
- Applications are connected.
- You can create a new Zap.
- Your first connection works correctly.

---

## Best Practices

- Use a strong password.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Organize connected apps.
- Remove unused connections.
- Review permissions regularly.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Skipping email verification.
- Connecting the wrong account.
- Ignoring security settings.
- Granting excessive permissions.
- Creating duplicate connections.

---

## Summary

A properly configured Zapier account provides the foundation for building secure, reliable, and scalable automation workflows.
