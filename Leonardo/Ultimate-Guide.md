# Leonardo Ultimate Guide

## Introduction

This guide combines everything you need to create professional AI images with Leonardo AI. By following this workflow, you can consistently generate high-quality, realistic, and creative artwork.

---

# Step 1: Define Your Goal

Ask yourself:

- What is the subject?
- What style do I want?
- What mood should the image have?
- Where is the scene located?

---

# Step 2: Build the Prompt

Use this formula:

Subject → Action → Environment → Lighting → Camera Angle → Style → Mood → Quality → Parameters

### Example

A futuristic female warrior standing on a rooftop overlooking a neon cyberpunk city, cinematic lighting, volumetric light rays, low-angle shot, ultra realistic, highly detailed, 8K.
