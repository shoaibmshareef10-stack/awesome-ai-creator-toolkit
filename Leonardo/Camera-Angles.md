# Leonardo Camera Angles

## Introduction

Camera angles play an important role in creating visually appealing AI images. The right angle improves storytelling, realism, and composition.

---

# Eye Level

Natural and realistic perspective.

---

# Close-up

Highlights facial expressions and fine details.

---

# Medium Shot

Shows the subject from the waist up while maintaining environmental context.

---

# Wide Shot

Captures the complete subject along with the surroundings.

---

# Low Angle

Makes the subject appear powerful and heroic.

---

# High Angle

Creates a feeling of vulnerability or scale.

---

# Bird's Eye View

A top-down perspective ideal for landscapes and city scenes.

---

# Worm's Eye View

An extreme low-angle shot for dramatic compositions.

---

# Drone Shot

Perfect for architecture, travel, and cinematic landscapes.

---

# Over-the-Shoulder

Useful for conversations and storytelling.

---

# Example Prompt

A futuristic warrior standing on a rooftop, cinematic lighting, low-angle shot, ultra realistic, highly detailed.

---

# Best Practices

- Match the angle with the story.
- Keep the main subject clearly visible.
- Use depth for cinematic compositions.
- Avoid unnecessary angle changes.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Random camera angles.
- Poor framing.
- Subject cropped incorrectly.
- Perspective that doesn't match the scene.
