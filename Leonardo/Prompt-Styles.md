# Leonardo Prompt Styles

## Introduction

Prompt styles help define the artistic direction, realism, and visual quality of your AI-generated images.

---

# Realistic

Natural lighting, detailed skin texture, lifelike colors, ultra realistic.

---

# Cinematic

Movie-quality composition, dramatic lighting, depth of field, realistic atmosphere.

---

# Anime

Japanese animation style, expressive characters, colorful backgrounds, clean line art.

---

# Fantasy

Magic, dragons, castles, glowing effects, mystical atmosphere.

---

# Cyberpunk

Neon lights, futuristic cities, holograms, advanced technology, rainy streets.

---

# Pixar Style

3D animated characters, expressive faces, colorful lighting, family-friendly look.

---

# Digital Art

Modern concept art, detailed illustrations, vibrant colors, professional quality.

---

# Steampunk

Victorian fashion, brass machinery, steam-powered technology, vintage atmosphere.

---

# Minimalist

Simple composition, clean background, soft colors, modern design.

---

# Example Prompt

A futuristic warrior standing in a neon city, cinematic lighting, cyberpunk style, ultra realistic, highly detailed.

---

# Best Practices

- Choose one primary style.
- Match the style with the subject.
- Combine lighting with the style.
- Keep prompts clean and descriptive.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Mixing too many styles.
- No clear artistic direction.
- Overloaded prompts.
- Ignoring lighting.
