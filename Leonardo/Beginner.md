# Leonardo Beginner Guide

## What is Leonardo AI?

Leonardo AI is an AI image generation platform that creates realistic images, illustrations, concept art, game assets, product designs, and marketing visuals from text prompts.

---

# Getting Started

1. Create a Leonardo AI account.
2. Choose an AI model.
3. Write a clear prompt.
4. Select image settings.
5. Generate the image.
6. Refine the prompt if needed.

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

Subject + Environment + Lighting + Camera + Style + Quality

---

# Example Prompt

A futuristic city at sunset, cinematic lighting, ultra realistic, 8K, highly detailed.

---

# Popular Styles

- Realistic
- Cinematic
- Anime
- Fantasy
- Pixar
- Cyberpunk
- Digital Art

---

# Tips

- Use clear and descriptive prompts.
- Mention lighting and camera angle.
- Keep prompts simple at first.
- Experiment with different styles.
- Save your best prompts.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Too many unrelated keywords.
- No lighting or style.
- Unrealistic expectations from a single prompt.
