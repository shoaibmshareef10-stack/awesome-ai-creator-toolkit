# Leonardo Best Practices

## Introduction

Following best practices helps you create high-quality, realistic, and professional AI images with Leonardo AI.

---

# Planning

- Define your goal before writing the prompt.
- Focus on one main subject.
- Choose an appropriate artistic style.

---

# Prompt Writing

- Start with the subject.
- Add the environment.
- Describe the lighting.
- Mention the camera angle.
- Specify the visual style.
- Include quality keywords.

---

# Composition

- Keep the subject clearly visible.
- Use balanced framing.
- Create depth with foreground and background.
- Avoid cluttered scenes.

---

# Lighting

- Match lighting to the mood.
- Use one primary lighting style.
- Keep shadows realistic.
- Maintain consistent light direction.

---

# Camera Angles

- Use Close-up for portraits.
- Use Wide Shot for landscapes.
- Use Low Angle for heroic scenes.
- Use Bird's Eye View for cityscapes.

---

# Style Consistency

- Keep colors consistent.
- Use one main art style.
- Avoid mixing unrelated styles.
- Maintain the same visual quality.

---

# Quality Tips

- Generate multiple variations.
- Compare results carefully.
- Improve prompts step by step.
- Save successful prompts.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Very short prompts.
- Too many conflicting keywords.
- No lighting description.
- Poor composition.
- Overcomplicated prompts.

---

# Pro Tips

- Build your own prompt library.
- Practice different styles regularly.
- Study photography and cinematography.
- Keep refining your prompts.
