# 📚 Knowledge

## Overview

Knowledge enables CrewAI agents to access external information beyond their built-in language model. By connecting to documents, databases, and other data sources, agents can provide more accurate, relevant, and up-to-date responses.

---

## What is Knowledge?

Knowledge is the information that agents use to complete tasks more effectively.

Knowledge sources may include:

- Documents
- PDFs
- Databases
- Websites
- Knowledge Bases
- APIs

---

## Why Use Knowledge?

Knowledge allows agents to:

- Answer domain-specific questions
- Access up-to-date information
- Reduce hallucinations
- Improve decision-making
- Generate more accurate responses

---

## Common Knowledge Sources

- Company Documentation
- Research Papers
- Product Manuals
- Internal Databases
- Web Content
- Vector Databases

---

## Typical Workflow

1. Connect a knowledge source.
2. Index or organize the data.
3. Retrieve relevant information.
4. Provide context to the agent.
5. Generate the final response.

---

## Benefits

- More accurate outputs
- Better contextual understanding
- Reduced misinformation
- Improved reliability
- Smarter AI workflows

---

## Best Practices

- Keep knowledge sources updated.
- Use trusted and verified data.
- Organize documents clearly.
- Remove outdated information.
- Test retrieval quality regularly.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Using outdated documents.
- Providing low-quality data.
- Ignoring retrieval accuracy.
- Overloading agents with unnecessary information.
- Not validating generated responses.

---

## Summary

Knowledge enhances CrewAI agents by giving them access to reliable external information, enabling more accurate, informed, and context-aware AI applications.
